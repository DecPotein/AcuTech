Introducing ACS!

What is ACS?

ACS is a classification model that aims to classify various types of acute coughs with convinience in mind!

When visiting hospitals, you often wait in line before you get an audience with a doctor. With ACS, you can get the results in just a matter of seconds!

It's far from perfect, but we plan to improve it more!

Currently, the software only classifies four types of acute coughs, these are:

  • Bronchitis

  • The Common Cold

  • Pertussis or Whooping Cough

  • Pneumonia

    • It also knows when you are healthy and just trying to cough!
